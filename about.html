    • About us.

    Welcome to Coffee Point, a family-owned and operated cafe that serves delicious coffee, tea, and baked goods. We are located in the heart of Quezon City, Philippines, and we are open from 7 am to 10 pm every day.

    Our cafe was founded in 2023 by Coco, a coffee enthusiast and baker who wanted to share her passion with the community. She started by selling her homemade cakes and cookies at local markets, and soon gained a loyal following of customers who loved her treats. With the help of her husband and two children, she decided to open her own cafe, where she could offer a wider variety of drinks and desserts.

    Flavors of Coffee and Pastry

    We use only the finest beans and ingredients to craft our drinks and desserts, and we serve them with a smile and a friendly chat.

    Whether you prefer a classic espresso, a creamy latte, a refreshing ice coffee, or a decadent mocha, we have something for everyone.
    Don't forget to try our signature pastries, such as our croissants, muffins, scones, and cakes. They are baked fresh every day and are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.
